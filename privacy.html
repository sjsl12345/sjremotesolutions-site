Privacy Notice

Last updated: 28 June 2026  ·  SJ Remote Solutions

1. Who we are

SJ Remote Solutions is an independent AI governance and compliance consultancy operating as a sole trader, registered with HMRC. Our registered address is in Wales, United Kingdom. We are the data controller for the personal data described in this notice.

Contact: stephanie@sjremotesolutions.co.uk

2. What personal data we collect and why

a) Registering your interest ("join the waitlist")
This website is currently a holding page for a platform still in development. If you leave your details via the waitlist form on our homepage or Insights page, we collect your email address, your agency name if you provide it, the page you submitted from, and the date and time of submission. We use this solely to notify you when the platform launches and to send occasional, relevant updates about AI compliance in recruitment. We do not use this information for any other purpose, and we do not sell or rent it to anyone.

b) Direct correspondence
If you email us directly, we hold that correspondence (and any personal data within it) for as long as reasonably necessary to respond to you and keep a record of the interaction.

c) Website usage data
We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies on this website. See our Cookie Notice for the limited, strictly necessary technical data involved in serving the site.

3. Our legal basis for processing

Where you register your interest with us, we rely on your consent (UK GDPR Article 6(1)(a)) — you actively choose to submit your details, and you can withdraw that consent at any time by emailing us or unsubscribing from any update we send. Where you contact us directly, we rely on our legitimate interest (Article 6(1)(f)) in responding to your enquiry.

4. Who we share your data with

We use a small number of service providers (sub-processors) to run this website and hold your data. We choose providers with strong data protection standards, and we do not permit them to use your data for their own purposes.

Supabase — our database and hosting provider. Waitlist submissions are stored in Supabase's UK (London) region.
Microsoft 365 — our email provider, used for any direct correspondence with you.
Cloudflare — hosts and serves this website's static pages.

We do not otherwise share your personal data with third parties, and we do not transfer it outside the UK.

5. How long we keep your data

Waitlist details are kept until the platform launches and you've had the chance to act on that update, or until you ask us to remove you, whichever is sooner. Direct correspondence is kept for as long as reasonably necessary, in line with standard professional record-keeping practice. You can ask us to delete your data at any time — see Section 6.

6. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to: access the personal data we hold about you; have inaccurate data corrected; ask us to delete your data; restrict or object to our processing; and receive your data in a portable format. To exercise any of these rights, email stephanie@sjremotesolutions.co.uk — we'll respond within one month.

We do not carry out any automated decision-making using the personal data described in this notice.

7. Complaints

If you're unhappy with how we've handled your personal data, please contact us first at stephanie@sjremotesolutions.co.uk so we can try to put it right. You also have the right to lodge a complaint directly with the UK's data protection regulator, the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO), at ico.org.uk.

8. Changes to this notice

We will update this notice when our processing activities change — including once the platform itself launches and this notice needs to cover the service directly. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. For material changes we will take reasonable steps to inform you directly.
