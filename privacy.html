Privacy Notice

Last updated: 28 June 2026  ·  SJ Remote Solutions

1. Who we are

SJ Remote Solutions is an independent AI governance and compliance consultancy operating as a sole trader, registered with HMRC. Our registered address is in Wales, United Kingdom. We are the data controller for the personal data described in this notice.

Contact: stephanie@sjremotesolutions.co.uk

2. What personal data we collect and why

a) Free AI Hiring Compliance Risk Calculator
When you choose to unlock your full risk assessment on our free calculator, we collect your email address. We use this to send you a copy of your indicative results and, from time to time, relevant compliance updates. The legal basis for this processing is your consent (UK GDPR Article 6(1)(a)). We also record the inputs you enter (number of candidates, AI usage level, safeguards in place) alongside your email address and estimated exposure band, so we can contextualise any follow-up conversation. This data is stored securely in our database hosted by Supabase (see section 6).

b) Booking a call
When you book a discovery call via our scheduling tool (Calendly), Calendly collects your name, email address and chosen time slot. Please refer to Calendly's privacy policy for how they process your data. We receive confirmation of booked appointments, which we use solely to prepare for and conduct your call. The legal basis is legitimate interests (UK GDPR Article 6(1)(f)) — specifically our mutual interest in holding the meeting you have requested.

c) Enquiries by email
If you email us directly, we will hold your name, email address and the content of your message for the purpose of responding to your enquiry and, where relevant, managing an ongoing client relationship. The legal basis is legitimate interests (UK GDPR Article 6(1)(f)).

d) Client engagements
Where you engage SJ Remote Solutions as a client, we will process personal data as set out in our client contract. We act as data processor for any personal data belonging to your candidates or staff that you share with us in the course of an engagement, and we will sign a Data Processing Agreement before any such data is shared.

3. How long we keep your data

Calculator leads: 24 months from collection, or until you ask us to delete your data.
Booking records: 12 months.
Email enquiries: 2 years from last contact.
Client data: as specified in the client contract, subject to any statutory retention obligations.

4. Your rights

Under UK GDPR you have the right to: access the personal data we hold about you; have inaccurate data corrected; have your data erased (in certain circumstances); restrict or object to processing; and data portability. If you provided consent, you may withdraw it at any time without affecting the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at stephanie@sjremotesolutions.co.uk. We will respond within one calendar month, as required by Article 12 UK GDPR. We operate a formal complaints process as required by the Data (Use and Access) Act 2025 — if you are unhappy with our response you may complain to the ICO at ico.org.uk.

5. Cookies

This website uses only essential cookies required for its operation. We do not use advertising, analytics or tracking cookies. See our Cookie Notice for details.

6. Data processors and transfers

We use the following third-party services that process personal data on our behalf:

  • Supabase Inc. (database hosting) — servers located in EU-West. Supabase is SOC 2 Type II certified.
  • Calendly LLC (appointment scheduling) — US-based, operating under standard contractual clauses for UK transfers.
  • Cloudflare Inc. (website hosting and CDN) — processes IP addresses as part of serving the website.

We do not sell, rent or share your personal data with any third party for marketing purposes.

7. Changes to this notice

We will update this notice when our processing activities change. The "last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. For material changes we will take reasonable steps to inform you directly.
