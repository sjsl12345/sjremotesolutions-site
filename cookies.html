What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to site owners.

Cookies we use

We keep our cookie use minimal and honest. This website uses only the following:

Cookie Purpose Type
__cf_bm Set by Cloudflare (our hosting provider) to distinguish humans from bots and protect the site from malicious traffic. Strictly necessary
calendly.* Set by Calendly on our booking page to enable the scheduling widget. Only present when you visit /booking. See Calendly's cookie policy. Functional (third-party)

We do not use advertising cookies, social media tracking pixels, Google Analytics or any behavioural profiling technology. We do not sell data derived from cookies to any third party.

Legal basis

Strictly necessary cookies do not require your consent under UK PECR as they are essential to providing the service you have requested. Where third-party functional cookies are set (e.g. Calendly on the booking page), these are set by the third party and governed by their own cookie policies.

Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Removing strictly necessary cookies may affect the functionality of the website. Most browsers allow you to refuse new cookies or delete existing ones — refer to your browser's help documentation for instructions.
